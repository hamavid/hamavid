I live in Seattle where I am usually making art, hanging out with my kids, or riding my bike around town.

The best way to contact me is by email: hamavid (at) gmail (dot) com

-Art-

I make paintings, drawings, and block prints. You can buy my notecards and stickers of my art in Seattle at Zoka or Lighthouse. Those are also available on Etsy along with originals. I sometimes display my art at cafés, laundry rooms, and law offices.
